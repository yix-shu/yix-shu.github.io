What Am I Up To Right Now?

September 8 2023


Just wrapped up my amazing internship experience at Blizzard, flew home to Seattle and spent quality time with friends and family. And then just got done enforcing at PAX West 2023! (Whew, what a busy tail end of the summer) It was my fourth year at PAX West -- I've gone 2018 as an attendee 2019 as Event Staff for SquareEnix, 2022 as Event Staff for Dire Wolf, and now in 2023 as an Enforcer, and every year it just gets better!
They built a new building called Summit, so now they use Summit, Arch, and Annex, which makes for easier crowd control, and it's nice to walk between the buildings to see more of the city around it.


Met really cool people (some from Riot Games, some I recognized from Blizzard, and new people I just talked to randomly or at the Kingsisle Booth -- screw you, Seattle Freeze!), played some really cool games that I'm really looking forward to, like Enotria: The Last Song, Arco, and Baldur's Gate 3 (already released, but I hadn't played it yet).
I splurged on plushies and a figurine from Wizard101, a deckbuilder, card battler, 3D MMORPG that I highly recommend trying out because it was my childhood game!


Overall, an amazing experience and just a reminder that one day I'll be back as an exhibitor >:D
