I designed and co-developed the entire game in Roblox Studio, while scripting in Lua, with a Bay Coding Club coworker. It follows the same structure as the Halloween Game, where it is round-based and comprised of mini-games. This time, there is a Snowball Fight, Snowbby, Ice Breakers, and Sword Fight. I designed the maps and the Spectator System, added music, designed the Snowbby so that we had various obstacles like marshmallows with automated up-down movement.
