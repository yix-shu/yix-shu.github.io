About Me

I am Yixing and I hail from Seattle, Washington, but I go to the University of Toronto for Mathematics! I'm taking Combinatorics, Graph Theory, and Classical Geometries, so wish me luck this year *cries*.
I love creating, gaming, teaching, gaming...mostly gaming. As you may have guessed, I am an avid gamer (not really, I mostly watch people play games #twitch and then go through a phase where I am obsessed with 1 that I just play for years on end).
